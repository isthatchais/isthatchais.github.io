White Space

Visual Hierarchy

  • IBM
  • IBM.com

I feel like IBM demonstrates good use of Visual Hierarchy. It draws your eye to different areas of the page in order based on text size and contrast.

PARC: Contrast

  • Adobe
  • Adobe.com

I feel like Adobe demonstrates good use of contrast. It draws attention to the creative cloud logo and the heavy black text.
