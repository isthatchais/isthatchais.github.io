Target Audience

Residents of Caribou, Franklin, and Bear Lake Counties, also people traveling through or planning to visit these locations for any reason.

Personas

  • 16 year old middle class highschool student who lives with her family in the area. She is wondering if she will have to drive through snow in the morning.
  • 40 year old middle class mother who just moved in to the area a month ago. She is wondering if she will need to bundle her kids up for their soccer game.

Senarios

  • A university student passing through the area on the way to Utah State thinking “when is this rain going to stop, or wait is it turning to snow?”.
  • A grandpa wondering if he will need to bring the extra ice chest so he has enough cold drinks to keep everyone happy on the fishing trip.
  • Friends going hunting in the area wondering what kind of gear they should pack for their trip.
  • A camper who can tell there is a storm rolling in wondering if he has time to make it back to camp before it starts to rain.
  • A family passing through on the way to visit their great aunt thinking if it keeps snowing they may shut down this road.
